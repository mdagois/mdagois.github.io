Biography

My name is Maximilien Dagois. I have programmed games for more than 15 years. I worked on titles like Final Fantasy XIV, Dragon Quest Builders and Pokémon Ranger. I have shipped a dozen games on PC, Mobile, DS, PS3, PS Vita, PS4 and Stadia.

I was born in France, near Paris. I lived close to eight years in Canada, first in Quebec City, then in Montreal. I now live in Tokyo, Japan, with my wife and two kids.

I work as a software engineer at Google, mostly dealing with graphics in Vulkan. In my spare time, I like to write books on how to program retro hardware.
