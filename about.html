Biography

Maximilien has programmed games for more than 15 years. He worked on titles like Final Fantasy XIV, Dragon Quest Builders and Pokémon Ranger. He has shipped a dozen games on PC, Mobile, DS, PS3, PS Vita, PS4 and Stadia.

Maximilien now works as a software engineer at Google. He lives with his wife and two kids in Tokyo, and you are most likely to find him playing on a good old gaming system.
